What I Do

Stucco

Stucco is a Portland and lime based product for the exterior of your home or office. Textures can be sprayed on or applied by hand by various techniques in a variety of textures. Stucco is applied to the exterior of your building and comes in a variety of textures. We will also match the color of the stucco to which ever color you wish for.

Venetian

Venetian plastering (also known as Polished Plastering) can be used to produce a depth of colour and texture for either a contemporary or classic feel. It can even be applied to ceilings or used as a substitute to stone flooring.

Tadelakt

Tadelakt is a stunning, versatile Moroccan plaster suitable for almost any internal or external wall, and a stylish alternative to traditional tiles in bathrooms or wetrooms. It is applied seamlessly, resulting in a beautifully smooth surface free from grout lines.

K’Rend

K'Rend in fact is ‘only’ a brand with a large range of different renders, manufactured by Kilwaughter, the chemical manufacturer parent company in Northern Ireland. K Rend traditionally produced ‘thick coat’ renders such as dash receivers and monocouche and has recently also ventured into the family of silicone render products.

About Me

“Innova Plaster” is a competent and proficient plastering business located in London, UK. The business was formed back in 2007 and started its history with working on various projects around the London area. The business has an exciting experience of working with commercial builders and other construction professionals as well as within the domestic homes market throughout the London and its surrounding area.
Formed by Ivan Kovtsuniak, the company has a unique passion for the quality, excellence and accuracy of the jobs they do. “Innova Plaster” has proven itself for using only high quality and exclusive materials.
Over the last 10 years “Innova Plaster” has worked on exclusive and same time challenging projects ranging from decorating restaurants and people’s homes.
Customer satisfaction is of the utmost importance for every plastering job undertaken; therefore the team is made up from highly skilled and devoted professionals who will ensure superb customer care and the ability to meet even the most demanding requirements.
With a high level of professionalism “Innova Plaster” will deliver excellent quality plastering jobs on time and to budget, ensuring accuracy and cleanliness of working area.
If you are looking for the reliable and trustworthy professional plasterers that you can entrust your project to, look no further.
